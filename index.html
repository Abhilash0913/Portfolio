A Little Bit About Me

I'm Abhilash Kodigalla

I'm a passionate and detail-oriented UI/UX Designer with 1 year of Intern experience specializing in Figma-based design. My work focuses on crafting intuitive and responsive interfaces for both web and mobile platforms.

I bring a strong understanding of user-centered design, interactive prototyping, and design systems to deliver engaging digital experiences. Throughout my journey, I've collaborated with cross-functional teams to translate complex ideas into clean, user-friendly interfaces.

From real-time healthcare and emergency platforms to e-commerce and educational dashboards, my projects reflect creativity, usability, and a deep commitment to problem-solving through design.

I'm always excited to explore new design trends, push creative boundaries, and contribute meaningfully to products that make a difference.
